Our Products

Abbington farm shop

We strive to be the best Scottish farm shop, proudly stocking mostly local and home-grown produce. Stocked full of local produce fresh from our fields or made by us. If we can’t make it ourselves, we’ll source it from the best local and regional suppliers.

What can you buy?

Abbington farm is, without doubt, a foodie's paradise. Why not try our freshly baked farmhouse bread, free-range eggs, fresh fruit and vegetables, high-quality homemade soups and oven-ready meals made by our kitchen team, as well as our fantastic range of craft beers and Scottish gins!

We serve the finest cuts including items from Buffalo Kitchen.We pride ourselves in the quality of our fresh meats and also the amazing prices that we can offer.

We have been producing homemade chutneys and fruit jams, follow family recipes and use traditional methods to make delicious preserves from fruit and vegetables grown, picked and preserved on the farm.

We have an array of great gifts for all the family, from our foodie favourite hampers to our farm-themed toys and treats for the children!

Take a sneaky look into our wonderful Farm Shop before you come and visit us.
