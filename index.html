We welcome everyone to Abbington Farm

You can be assured of a very warm welcome, the chance to try out new experiences, play on our farm and enjoy delicious farm produce.

Here we pride ourselves on our huge range of activities for all, outdoor play equipment and farm animals to meet, our cafe ABBINGTON serving delicious, locally-sourced food.

The farm shop has a wide range of fresh products, from jams and preserves to eggs and bacon.

You can buy fresh vegetables and fruits grown in our garden.

The Abbington Farm promises a great day out for all amilies, garden enthusiasts, travellers looking for a quality place to stop
