Garden and fields tour

Take a guided stroll through a working Abbington farm. See where and discover how fruits and vegetables are grown and picked.

  • Walk in the garden
  • Pick your own produce from the garden and field to take home with you!
  • Play with farm tools, such as a pitch fork or shovel
  • Make picnic

Small farm zoo

Visit the animals, see how they live in their natural habitat, and get the chance to feed them, too!

Some of the farm animals we have on show at present include:

  • horses
  • goats
  • chickens
  • sheep
  • pigs
  • lambs

Play Park

We offer the following activities in our wonderful Park, fun for all ages!

Lots of your favourite play equipment including:

  • Small observation wheel
  • The Pond
  • Nest Swings
  • Exciting slides
  • Aerial runway
  • Toddlers play area

Abbington Cafe

At our cafe we take great pride in providing fresh, seasonal food that's grown on the farm or locally sourced where possible

Our cafe is open for walk in visits, so please stop by to enjoy our homemade hot meals or simply coffee and cake!

Farm Shop

Handmade, homegrown, local, fresh, unusual, delicious...

We pride ourselves on great quality and excellent customer service...Offering the freshest fruit and vegetables straight from our own fields, or sourced from local suppliers, the best locally sourced meats, a huge selection of cheeses, home baked bread and handmade cakes as well as much much more...and all under one roof!
